Avocado Banana Dessert Salad

Ingredients

  • Condensed Milk or Sugar
  • Evap Milk or Powdered milk (with some water)
  • Avocados
  • Banana
  • Ice

Steps

  1. Chop the bananas and avocados into small peices (or to your liking), then set aside in a bowl
  2. Mix the Evap Milk or Powdered milk (with some water) with Condensed Milk or Sugar. Add as much Condensed Milk or Sugar as you like
  3. Add the ice, avocados and bananas to the milk
  4. Serve and Enjoy
