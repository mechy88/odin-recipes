Cucumber Salad

Ignore the red onions (I don't like those on my cucumber salad)

Ingredients

  • Cucumbers
  • sugar
  • Vinegar
  • Black pepper

Steps

  1. Peel the cucumbers, then chop to about 0.2 cm thick then set aside on a serving platter. Chill in fridge if there's time
  2. In a bowl, mix sugar, vinegar, and black pepper. This will be the dressing
  3. Serve chilled and enjoy!
