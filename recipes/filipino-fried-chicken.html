Filipino Fried Chicken

Ingredients

  • Fish Sauce
  • Garlic
  • Black pepper
  • Calamansi juice (or some other citrus of choice)
  • Flour
  • Chicken
  • Frying oil

Steps

  1. Marinate the chicken in fish sauce, garlic, black pepper, and calamansi juice for 45 mins - 1 hr and 45 mins
  2. Prepare a bowl with flour
  3. Take out the chicken, pat dry, and cover with flour
  4. Heat some oil. When the oil is hot enough, fry the chicken. Tip: Do not overcrowd, cook in small batches to maintain oil temp.
  5. Serve hot and enjoy!
